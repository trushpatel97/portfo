005 : Story Spinner

Summary

There are a lot of books in this world. However, some genres might not have as many books as you'd like to read. Therefore, there is some limitations in the amount of books there are to read. Wouldn't it be cool to enjoy your most favorite genres and never run out of what to read? If your favorite types of book are science fiction, and just want a quick read, have a program generate one for you on the spot!

With this project Story Spinner, it is trained to make the program output random stories related to your category preference. It is trained by feeding the program thousands of text related to your category with datasets that are available online. The best part? It is not limited to just books. There are millions of datasets such as: pop songs, political speeches, yelp reviews, etc. With this being said, you have the ability to have the program generate a song, write a speech, and write reviews for you respectively. Simply give the program a seed to work off with in the input box, or just click generate and watch it make its own!
