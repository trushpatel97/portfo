002 : Cruelty-Free Mousetrap

Summary

In this project, I worked in a group of three students to make a mousetrap that does not harm mouses. The project was inspired by a student in the group, who wanted to find a solution to get rid of mouses where he worked. We used an Arduino, which allows us to use a physical piece of hardware, and program it to do different things.

For the project, we made a trap-box from scratch. A shoe box was taken and a small opening door was cut for the mice to fall in. How did the door get triggered? The door was triggered through a sensor. Once the sensor detected that the mouse has stepped on the door, it quickly opens it, and closes it. Assuring the mice has no way out...and of course, not be harmed. This project was later used by a group member to address the mouse problem he encounterd at his work!

Contribution

  • Assembled sensors and wires for the project through breadboarding
  • Programmed a servo to act as a trap door for the mice
